10/27/25: First Drive with the Chiron

Finally got behind the wheel of the Bugatti Chiron today. The 8.0-liter quad-turbo W16 engine is a masterpiece — 1,500 horsepower that feels infinite. The acceleration is so smooth, yet so brutal, it almost bends reality. Every gear shift felt like a surge of controlled chaos.

On the open stretch, I touched 330 km/h before I even realized it. Stability, precision, dominance — there’s simply nothing like it. The Chiron doesn’t drive; it conquers.

10/26/25: Detailing Day

Spent the day detailing the Chiron — ceramic coat, microfiber buff, and a polish that brought out that deep midnight blue. The reflections under the garage lights are hypnotic. Every curve and carbon fiber weave reminds me this isn’t just a car, it’s a work of art.

Tomorrow, planning to install a custom titanium exhaust tip. A little more roar, a little more presence — just how a Chiron should be.
